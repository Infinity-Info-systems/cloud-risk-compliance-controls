# Compliance Alignment

## Purpose

Compliance alignment maps cloud controls to the regulatory, contractual, and policy requirements the organization must meet.

## Alignment Activities

- identify applicable frameworks
- map controls to requirements
- confirm evidence sources
- review control testing frequency
- track gaps and exceptions

## Common Frameworks

- HIPAA
- SOC 2
- FedRAMP
- internal security policies
- customer contractual requirements

## Evidence To Collect

- control matrix
- policy documents
- control test results
- evidence artifacts
- exception approvals
