# Cloud Risk Governance

## Purpose

Cloud risk governance defines how risk is identified, assessed, owned, and remediated across the cloud estate.

## Governance Principles

- risk must have an owner
- material gaps must be visible
- exceptions must be time-bound
- remediation should be sequenced by exposure and dependency
- leadership should review unresolved high-risk items regularly

## Risk Review Questions

- What risks are currently open?
- Which risks affect regulated workloads?
- Which risks have no owner?
- Which exceptions are overdue?

## Evidence To Collect

- risk register entries
- control exceptions
- remediation plans
- ownership assignments
- review meeting notes
