# Audit Readiness Model

## Purpose

The audit readiness model helps teams keep evidence, control testing, and exception handling in a condition that is ready for review at any time.

## Readiness Signals

- evidence is current and retrievable
- controls are mapped to owners
- exceptions are approved and tracked
- review cycles are documented
- remediation items are measured to closure

## Audit Questions

- Can the team show the control working?
- Can the team show who owns the control?
- Can the team show the last review or test?
- Can the team show how exceptions are handled?
