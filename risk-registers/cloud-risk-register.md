# Cloud Risk Register

## Purpose

Track cloud risks that affect governance, security, reliability, and compliance.

## Suggested Fields

- risk ID
- risk description
- owner
- likelihood
- impact
- status
- due date
- mitigation notes
- evidence link
- next review date

## Example Risks

- insufficient logging retention
- unclear privileged access review cadence
- untested recovery assumptions
- incomplete third-party review coverage

## Register Guidance

Keep the register short enough to review, but specific enough that each risk can be acted on without extra context.
